## Conditionals

| Type | If clause | Main clause | Meaning | Example |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------|---------|
| **Zero** | Present simple | Present simple | Always true | `If you heat ice, it melts.` |
| **First** | Present simple | will + verb | Real possibility | `If it rains, I will stay home.` |
| **Second** | Past simple | would + verb | Unreal / imaginary | `If I won the lottery, I would travel.` |
| **Third** | Past perfect | would have + past participle | Past impossible | `If you had studied, you would have passed.` |

### Quick Tips
- **Zero** → facts, habits
- **First** → real future
- **Second** → hypothetical now/future
- **Third** → regret, past mistake
