## have got to do sth

You use have got to when you are saying that something is necessary or must happen in the way stated. In informal American English, the 'have' is sometimes omitted

## examples:

- **I have got to go now.** = الان باید بروم.
- **She has got to study harder.** = او باید سختتر درس بخواند.
- **We have got to finish this today.** = باید امروز این را تمام کنیم.
- **See, you got to work very hard.** ('have' omitted)
