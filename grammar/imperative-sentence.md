## Imperative Sentence

| Use | Example |
|-----|---------|
| **Commands** | `Close the door.` |
| **Instructions** | `Press the button.` |
| **Advice** | `Take a break.` |
| **Offers / invitations** | `Have a seat.` |
| **Warnings** | `Be careful!` |

### Form
- **Base verb** (no subject)
- Negative: **Don't** + base verb → `Don't run.`

### With "let's"
- `Let's go.` (suggestion including yourself)
- Negative: `Let's not argue.`
