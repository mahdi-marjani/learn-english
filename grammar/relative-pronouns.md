## Relative Pronouns Cheat Sheet

| Pronoun | Use for | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **who** | people (subject) | `The girl who called is my sister.` |
| **whom** | people (object) | `The man whom I met was friendly.` |
| **which** | things, animals | `The car which broke down is old.` |
| **that** | people OR things | `The book that I read was good.` <br> `The man that helped me is kind.` <br> `The phone that has no battery is mine.` |
| **whose** | possession (people, things, animals) | `The woman whose bag was lost is upset.` <br> `The house whose roof is red looks nice.` |

---

### Quick Tips

- **Who** → subject for people (he/she/they)
- **Whom** → object for people (him/her/them) – less common in casual English
- **Which** → only things/animals
- **That** → anyone or anything (casual, but not for possession)
- **Whose** → shows ownership (like “his/her/its/their”)
